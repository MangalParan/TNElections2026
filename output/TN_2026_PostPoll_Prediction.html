⚙️ Post-Poll Adjustment Factors (Applied to Pre-Poll Predictions)

🔻 Factors Favoring AIADMK+ (Upward Revision)

  • Erode +13.19 pp surge — PMK + AIADMK's Vanniyar/OBC core base mobilized
  • Palaniswami's confident reaction to high turnout (post-poll statement)
  • Northern TN rural surge — AIADMK historically stronger in Vellore/TVM belt
  • PMK full solidarity with AIADMK in NDA — Vanniyar block intact
  • Anti-incumbency factor — DMK's 5-year rule, debt concerns, NEET politics
  • Congress leaders' regret on not joining TVK (implies TVK pulled Congress voters away from SPA)

🔺 Factors Favoring SPA/DMK (Maintains Lead)

  • Women voters higher turnout (85.76%) — DMK welfare schemes for women very popular (₹1000/month scheme)
  • Madurai + Thoothukudi — Southern TN is DMK stronghold, good turnout
  • TVK vote primarily hurts AIADMK — Vijay's base overlaps with anti-DMK, not pro-DMK
  • Sasikala faction (81 seats) splits AIADMK core vote by 3-5% in target constituencies
  • 2024 Lok Sabha sweep (39/39) — DMK alliance's organizational machinery proven dominant
  • Dravidian governance record — Breakfast scheme, free uniforms, bus passes popular
  • NTK's 5.8% vote — Seeman draws primarily from anti-ADMK Tamil nationalist pool

🤝 Alliance Performance Analysis

🔴 SPA (DMK Alliance) — Why 149 Seats?

  • Women voter edge: 85.76% women turnout; DMK's ₹1000/month scheme hugely popular
  • Southern TN sweep: Tirunelveli, Thoothukudi, Ramanathapuram, Kanyakumari — Nadar belt
  • Coastal constituencies: Fishing community — DMK's food distribution, boat schemes
  • SC/ST seats: VCK+DMK combination near-unbeatable in reserved constituencies
  • 2024 Lok Sabha mandate: 42.12% vote → organizational momentum
  • Alliance arithmetic: INC+DMDK+VCK+CPI+CPI(M)+IUML+MDMK all solid
  • NTK vote: 5.8% Seeman votes damage AIADMK more than DMK
  • Sriperumbudur, Madhavaram: Huge voter additions favor incumbent DMK

🟢 AIADMK+ (NDA) — Why 72 Seats?

  • Erode/Salem/Dharmapuri: Gounder belt surges +9–13 pp — AIADMK's rural core
  • PMK intact: Vanniyar community (10–11% in North TN) fully behind AIADMK+
  • Anti-incumbency: 5-year DMK rule; state debt; NEET issue voter fatigue
  • EPS personal popularity: Edappadi groundwork; 234 constituency tours
  • Northern TN rural surge: Vellore, Tiruvannamalai, Dharmapuri
  • TVK cannibalization limited: Vijay draws primarily urban; rural AIADMK intact
  • BJP vote consolidation: BJP+AIADMK combination avoids anti-NDA split
  • Thevar community: Sivaganga/Madurai AIADMK pockets hold 2–3 seats

🟡 TVK (Vijay) — Why Only ~9 Seats?

  • First election party: No organizational depth; booth agent shortage
