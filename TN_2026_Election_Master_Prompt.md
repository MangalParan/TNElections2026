```

Your task is to predict the outcome of the 2026 Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Election (polling: April 23, 2026; results: May 4, 2026; 234 seats; majority: 118; 5.67 crore registered voters).

### 2026 Alliance Configuration

- 2021 result: Won 159 seats (DMK 133 + allies), 44.06% vote share

**AIADMK+ (NDA — Opposition):**
- AIADMK (172 seats), BJP (33), PMK (18), AMMK/TTV Dhinakaran (11)
- CM candidate: Edappadi K. Palaniswami (EPS)
- Key losses: Lost DMDK, OPS, Sasikala, Sengottaiyan. Organization weakened post-Jayalalithaa.
- 2021 result: Won 75 seats (ADMK 66, BJP 4, PMK 5), 37.30% vote share
- 2024 Lok Sabha: ADMK contested alone (20.46%), BJP alone (11.24%). Combined NDA: ~34.7%. Won 0 seats.

**TVK (Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam — Actor Vijay, New Entrant):**
- Contesting all 234 seats solo. First-ever election.
- CM candidate: Vijay (popular Tamil film actor)
- No electoral track record. Star power + youth appeal. Karur crowd crush tragedy tempered enthusiasm.
- Expected to draw votes: ~60% from ADMK base, ~25% from DMK, ~15% from others

**NTK (Naam Tamilar Katchi — Seeman):**

### Key Factors to Weigh
1. **Anti-incumbency pattern**: DMK never won consecutive elections. Alternation holds 67%.
2. **2024 LS sweep**: SPA won 39/39 — strongest pre-Assembly validation ever for any TN ruling party.
3. **TVK vote splitting**: New third force expected to drain 8-15% votes, primarily from ADMK (60:25:15 ratio).
4. **ADMK organizational weakness**: Lost Jayalalithaa, OPS, Sasikala, DMDK, Sengottaiyan.
5. **DMK welfare delivery**: Breakfast scheme, Kalaignar insurance, housing — tangible beneficiary base.
6. **Opposition fragmentation**: Non-DMK vote split across ADMK+, TVK, NTK, Sasikala, PMK(R).

## YOUR TASK

Using the above data, perform a rigorous multi-model election prediction:

### Model 1: Historical Alternation Pattern
Analyze the 67% alternation rate. Note DMK's 0/5 consecutive win record. But also note ADMK won back-to-back (1977-80-84, 2011-16). Assess whether current conditions (ADMK weakness, TVK) make this pattern unreliable.

### Model 2: Swing Analysis (2024 LS → 2026 Assembly)
Convert 2024 LS vote shares to 2026 Assembly projections. Account for: TVK emergence drawing from both camps (60:25:15), united NDA vs split 2024, Sasikala drain on ADMK, LS-to-Assembly typically being a different electorate.

### Model 3: Opinion Poll Aggregation
Weight the 4 polls by sample size (60%) and recency (40%). Calculate weighted average seats and standard deviation. Note the massive divergence.

### Model 4: Incumbency Factor Scorecard
Rate DMK's incumbency advantage on: LS sweep, welfare delivery, alliance management, organizational strength vs anti-incumbency factors: historical pattern, inflation, corruption allegations, liquor policy. Calculate net score.

### Model 5: Vote Share → Seat Conversion
Use a modified cube law (power = 2.5) for multi-corner FPTP TN contests. Model scenarios: Close 3-way (39/36/14), DMK lead (42/34/12), DMK dominant (45/32/10), ADMK lead (36/40/13).

### Model 6: Alliance Arithmetic & Caste Matrix
Calculate each alliance's core vote, apply transfer efficiency (SPA 85%, ADMK+ 75%, TVK 90%), add new voter attraction, subtract leakage to TVK/Sasikala. Analyze caste community alignment.

### Model 7: Composite Ensemble
Combine all 6 models with weights: Opinion Polls 30%, Alliance Arithmetic 20%, Swing 15%, Incumbency 15%, Historical 10%, Vote→Seat 10%. Calculate final seat prediction with confidence intervals and win probability.

## OUTPUT FORMAT

1. Each model's prediction in a table (seats range + vote share for SPA, ADMK+, TVK, Others)
2. Final ensemble prediction with:
   - Point estimate + range for each alliance
   - Win probability for each outcome (SPA majority, ADMK+ majority, Hung, TVK kingmaker)
   - Predicted CM
3. Five scenarios with probabilities:
   - DMK Comfortable Win, DMK Narrow Win, Hung Assembly, ADMK+ Comeback, DMK Landslide
4. Top 5 risk factors that could invalidate the prediction
5. Why DMK may or may not break the anti-incumbency pattern
6. Regional breakdown: Chennai, Northern TN, Western TN, Southern TN, Delta, Kongu Belt

Be rigorous. Show your calculations. Label predictions vs facts. Provide confidence levels (High/Medium/Low). Do NOT speculate without data backing.
```
